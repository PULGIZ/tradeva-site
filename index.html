Platform Features

Everything you need to trade crypto

Tradeva brings together secure P2P trading, fast transfers, and institutional-grade wallet storage in one premium platform.

Secure P2P Trading

Trade directly with other users through a secure escrow process. Funds are held safely until both parties confirm — giving you peace of mind on every trade.

Fast Transactions

Quick and reliable crypto transfers designed for speed without compromising security. Whether buying, selling, or moving funds — Tradeva keeps things smooth.

Secure Wallet Storage

Store your crypto using a wallet system built with security at its core. Separate hot and cold wallets give you flexible access and enhanced long-term protection.

Global Platform

Tradeva is designed for users around the world. No matter where you are — find trading partners, manage your crypto, and trade with confidence.

How It Works

Simple. Clear. Secure.

Getting started with Tradeva takes minutes. Four steps and you're trading peer-to-peer with confidence.

01
Create an Account

Sign up with your email and set up your secure Tradeva profile in just a few minutes.

02
Complete Verification

Verify your identity when verification becomes available — keeping the platform safe for everyone.

03
Fund Your Wallet

Deposit or receive cryptocurrency into your Tradeva wallet. Hot and cold storage options available.

Wallet Storage

Two wallets. One platform.

Tradeva gives you both a hot wallet for everyday trading and a cold wallet for long-term protection — all in one place.

  Hot Wallet
Everyday Access

Your hot wallet is always connected and ready — designed for quick trades and fast transfers. Access your funds instantly whenever you need to act on the market.

  • ✓Instant fund access for trading
  • ✓Optimized for P2P transactions
  • ✓Real-time balance updates
  • ✓Send and receive crypto quickly
  Cold Wallet
Enhanced Protection

Your cold wallet is designed for enhanced protection and long-term storage. Assets stored here are isolated from active trading, adding an extra layer of security.

  • ✓Isolated from trading environments
  • ✓Designed for long-term holding
  • ✓Enhanced security architecture
  • ✓Ideal for large asset storage
Security First

Built with trust at the core

Security isn’t a feature at Tradeva — it’s the foundation. Every part of the platform is designed with your safety in mind.

Escrow-Protected Trading

Every P2P trade passes through our escrow system. Funds are held securely until both parties confirm, eliminating counterparty risk.

Secure Wallet Architecture

Tradeva’s wallet system separates assets by access level. Hot and cold wallets are purpose-built for different security needs.

User-First Design

Security decisions are made from the user’s perspective — simple interfaces that don’t make you choose between convenience and safety.

Reliable Transaction Process

Tradeva’s transaction process is consistent and reliable. Every transfer follows the same trusted flow from start to finish.

Transparent Operations

Clear status indicators and activity logs keep you in full control of your trades and funds at all times.

Global Safety Standards

Tradeva is being built to meet high safety standards across jurisdictions, so users worldwide can trade with confidence.
