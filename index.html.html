Platform Features

Everything you need to trade crypto

Tradeva brings together secure P2P trading, fast transfers, and institutional-grade wallet storage in one premium platform.

🤝
Secure P2P Trading

Trade directly with other users through a secure escrow process. Funds are held safely until both parties confirm the transaction — giving you peace of mind on every trade.

⚡
Fast Transactions

Quick and reliable crypto transfers designed for speed without compromising security. Whether you're buying, selling, or moving funds, Tradeva keeps things smooth.

🔐
Secure Wallet Storage

Store your cryptocurrency using a wallet system built with security in mind. Separate hot and cold wallets give you flexible access and enhanced protection for long-term holdings.

🌍
Global Platform

Tradeva is designed for users around the world. No matter where you are, you can access the platform, find trading partners, and manage your crypto with ease.

How It Works

Simple. Clear. Secure.

Getting started with Tradeva takes minutes. Four simple steps and you're trading peer-to-peer with confidence.

01
Create an Account

Sign up with your email and set up your secure Tradeva profile in just a few minutes.

02
Complete Verification

Verify your identity when verification becomes available — a key part of keeping the platform safe for everyone.

03
Fund Your Wallet

Deposit or receive cryptocurrency into your Tradeva wallet. Both hot and cold storage options are available.

04
Trade via Escrow

Find a trading partner and execute trades through our secure escrow system. Funds release only when both sides confirm.
